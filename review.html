GAME REVIEWS

Honest and in-depth reviews from our expert gamers

Call of Duty: Warzone

(4.5/5)

An intense battle royale experience with smooth gameplay and excellent graphics. The Verdansk map offers diverse combat scenarios that keep matches feeling fresh even after hundreds of hours.

  • Excellent gunplay and weapon customization
  • Large map with varied environments
  • Regular content updates keep the game fresh
  • Cross-play support expands the player base
PC, PlayStation, Xbox
- Reviewed by MZ Dhilu

Fortnite

(4/5)

Creative building mechanics and constant updates keep the game fresh. The crossover events with popular franchises are impressive, though the building mechanics have a steep learning curve.

  • Unique building mechanic sets it apart
  • Frequent content updates and collaborations
  • Colorful, appealing visual style
  • Free-to-play with fair monetization
PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Mobile
- Reviewed by MZ Rayyutan

Apex Legends

(5/5)

Outstanding character abilities and team-based gameplay. The movement mechanics are some of the best in the genre, with fluid sliding and climbing that makes traversal feel exhilarating.

  • Excellent character-based gameplay
  • Superb movement mechanics
  • Innovative ping system for communication
  • Regular seasonal content
PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Switch
- Reviewed by MZ Bruno

eFootball 2023

(3.5/5)

Improved gameplay mechanics from previous versions but still lacks in content compared to competitors. The free-to-play model makes it accessible, but microtransactions are somewhat aggressive.

  • Realistic football gameplay
  • Free-to-play model
  • Improved graphics and animations
  • Limited game modes at launch
PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Mobile
- Reviewed by MZ Dhilu

The Witcher 3: Wild Hunt

(5/5)

A masterpiece of storytelling and world-building. The expansive world is filled with meaningful quests and memorable characters, making it one of the best RPGs ever created.

  • Incredible storytelling and characters
  • Massive, detailed open world
  • Meaningful choices that affect the world
  • Outstanding expansion content
PC, PlayStation, Xbox, Switch
- Reviewed by MZ Shammu

VALORANT

(4/5)

A tactical shooter that successfully blends elements from CS:GO with hero-based abilities. Precise gunplay is rewarded, and the unique agent abilities add strategic depth to each round.

  • Tactical, skill-based gameplay
  • Diverse agent roster with unique abilities
  • Precise and satisfying gunplay
  • Regular updates and balancing
PC
- Reviewed by Kingcobra
